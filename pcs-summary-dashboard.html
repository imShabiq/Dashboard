Step 1
Enter your data
Go to the Dashboard tab. At the bottom, use the "Enter / Update Data" form:
  • Select the outlet (Angsana, Ward Place, Trizen, Monarch)
  • Choose the year (2024, 2025, 2026)
  • Pick the month
  • Type the PCS value (number of pieces)
  • Click Save Entry
The chart updates instantly. Your data is saved automatically in your browser.
Step 2
Filter the chart
Use the filter buttons above the chart:
  • Outlet filters — click an outlet name to see only that outlet, or "All outlets" to see all four at once. You can select multiple outlets too.
  • Year filters — toggle 2024, 2025, and/or 2026 on/off to compare years side by side.
The summary cards at the top update to reflect your selected filters.
Step 3
Review all data
Click the All Data tab to see every entry in a table. You can:
  • Filter by outlet or year using the dropdowns
  • Edit any entry by clicking Edit
  • Delete individual entries with Delete
  • Clear all data with Clear All Data
Step 4
Export to Excel
Click the ⬇ Export Excel button (top right, always visible). This downloads a .xlsx file with:
  • A Summary sheet — all 4 outlets × 12 months × 3 years in a grid
  • An All Data sheet — every raw entry in a table
Open in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.

Data storage
Where is data saved?
Your data is saved in your browser's local storage. This means:
  • ✅ Data persists when you close and reopen the file
  • ✅ No internet connection needed
  • ⚠️ Data is tied to this browser on this computer
  • 💡 Use Export Excel regularly to back up your data
